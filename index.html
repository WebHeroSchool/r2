SUN SHINES ON TRIATHLON

The sun finally shone on the Three Kings School Final triathlon. After many days of rain the children of Room 9 got to bask in the sun as they did their swim, cycle and run.

The boys of Room 9 started of the triathlon with a four width swim. Next the firls pushed themselves through the same course.

Racing of to the bottom court the boys jumped onto the bikes for four circuirs before their run.

Off the bikes, and it was the girls turn. Four laps was done in record times.

The field track was run at speed by both girls and boys.

Crossing the finish line first for the boys was Joshua Sharp whith a time of 2.04.

The girls were fast on the heels of the boys and Siobhan Oveinikovas won the race 1 second in front of top contender Ashleigh Commons.

Commons said of her race "I tried to beat her, but on the day she had more in the tank that I did."

A great afternoon for the students of Room 9.
